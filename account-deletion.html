Account Deletion Guide

Last Updated: November 13, 2024

We respect your right to delete your account and personal data at any time. This guide explains how to permanently delete your Eko account, what data will be removed, and what to expect during the deletion process.

‚ö†Ô∏è Important Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your conversation history, progress, achievements, and learning data will be permanently deleted. Please ensure you want to proceed before deleting your account.

Method 1: Delete Account In-App (Recommended)

The fastest and easiest way to delete your account is directly through the Eko app:

  1. Open the Eko App

    Launch Eko on your device and ensure you are signed in to your account.

  2. Go to Profile Screen

    Tap the profile icon or navigate to your profile/account settings from the main menu.

  3. Access Account Settings

    Look for "Account Settings" or "Manage Account" option in your profile.

  4. Select "Delete Account"

    Scroll down to find the "Delete Account" or "Delete My Data" button.

  5. Confirm Your Decision

    Read the confirmation message carefully. You may be asked to:

    • Re-authenticate (sign in again for security)
    • Type "DELETE" or confirm with a checkbox
    • Provide an optional reason for deletion (helps us improve)
  6. Final Confirmation

    Tap "Delete My Account Permanently" to complete the process.

  7. Deletion Complete

    You will see a confirmation message, and you'll be signed out immediately. Your data deletion begins processing right away.

‚úÖ Deletion Timeline: Your account will be deleted immediately, but the complete removal of all data from backups may take up to 30 days.

Method 2: Request Deletion via Email

If you cannot access the app or prefer to request deletion via email, follow these steps:

  1. Send Email Request

    Email us at: myh.public@gmail.com

  2. Include Required Information

    In your email, please provide:

    • Subject: "Account Deletion Request"
    • Your Email: The email address associated with your Eko account

    • Verification: For security, we may ask you to verify your identity
  3. Wait for Confirmation

    We will process your request within 48 hours and send you a confirmation email once your account has been deleted.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

‚úÖ Data That Will Be Deleted:

  • Account Information: Email, name, profile photo, user ID
  • All Conversations: Complete conversation history and transcripts
  • Learning Progress: XP points, levels, achievements, badges
  • Streaks: Daily streak records and history
  • Vocabulary Data: Saved words, flashcard progress, spaced repetition data
  • Settings & Preferences: App settings, notification preferences
  • Authentication Data: Your account will be removed from Firebase Authentication

‚ùå Data That May Be Retained (Anonymized):

For legal, security, and operational purposes, we may retain certain anonymized data:

  • Crash Reports: Anonymous crash data (no personal identifiers) - retained for 90 days
  • Analytics: Aggregated, anonymized usage statistics - retained for 90 days
  • Backup Data: Data in system backups may take up to 30 days to be completely purged
  • Legal Records: If required by law (e.g., tax records, legal investigations)

Important: Anonymized data cannot be linked back to you or your account and does not contain any personally identifiable information.

Consequences of Account Deletion

Before you delete, please understand:
  • Cannot be undone: Account deletion is permanent
  • All progress lost: You cannot recover your learning history, achievements, or XP
  • No data export: Make sure to export any data you want to keep before deletion
  • Cannot reuse email: You may need to wait 30 days before creating a new account with the same email
  • No refunds: If you had any paid subscriptions (future feature), no refunds will be provided

Alternatives to Account Deletion

If you're not sure about permanently deleting your account, consider these alternatives:

Option 1: Delete Individual Conversations

You can delete specific conversations without deleting your entire account:

  • Go to Conversation History in the app
  • Swipe left on a conversation (or tap menu)
  • Select "Delete" to remove that conversation

Option 2: Sign Out and Stop Using

Simply sign out and stop using the app. Your data will remain stored but inactive. You can return anytime by signing in again.

Option 3: Contact Support

If you're having issues with the app, please contact our support team first. We may be able to help resolve your concerns without needing to delete your account.

Re-Creating Your Account

If you change your mind after deleting your account:

  • You can create a new account using the same email address (after 30-day backup purge)
  • Your new account will start fresh with no previous data
  • Previous achievements, XP, and conversation history cannot be restored

  • Right to Data Portability: Request a copy of your data before deletion
  • Right to Object: Object to data processing

To exercise these rights, please contact us at myh.public@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days as required by GDPR.
