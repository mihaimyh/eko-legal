Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 13, 2024

FlickAI ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Eko mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our App.

Important: By using Eko, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account using Google Sign-In or Apple Sign-In, we collect:

  • Email address: For account identification and communication
  • Display name: To personalize your experience
  • Profile photo: If provided by your sign-in provider
  • Unique user ID: Generated by Firebase Authentication

1.2 Conversation Data

To provide our educational services, we collect:

  • Voice recordings: Audio of your conversations with the AI tutor (processed in real-time, not permanently stored)
  • Conversation transcripts: Text versions of your conversations
  • AI responses: Messages generated by our AI tutor
  • Conversation metadata: Date, time, duration, and topic of conversations

1.3 Learning Progress Data

We track your learning progress to provide personalized education:

  • XP (Experience Points): Points earned through learning activities
  • Achievements: Milestones completed and badges earned
  • Streaks: Daily usage patterns and consecutive learning days
  • Vocabulary progress: Words learned and flashcard review history
  • Performance metrics: Pronunciation accuracy and conversation quality

1.4 Technical and Usage Data

We automatically collect certain technical information:

  • Device information: Device type, operating system version, app version
  • Usage data: App features used, session duration, interaction patterns
  • Crash reports: Anonymous technical data when the app crashes (via Firebase Crashlytics)
  • Performance data: App performance metrics (via Firebase Performance Monitoring)

1.5 Data We Do NOT Collect

For your privacy, we explicitly do NOT collect:

  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Contact lists or address books
  • Photos or media from your device (except your profile photo if provided via sign-in)
  • Browsing history or data from other apps
  • Payment information (app is currently free)

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Providing Educational Services

  • Generate AI-powered conversation responses tailored to your skill level
  • Provide real-time pronunciation and grammar feedback
  • Track learning progress and display achievements
  • Personalize lesson content based on your performance

2.2 App Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve features and user experience
  • Identify and fix technical issues and bugs
  • Monitor app performance and stability
  • Develop new educational features and content

2.3 Communication

  • Send important updates about the App or your account
  • Respond to your support requests and inquiries
  • Notify you about new features (with your consent)

2.4 Legal Compliance

  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Protect our rights and the safety of our users

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Firebase Services (Google)

We use Firebase services provided by Google LLC:

  • Firebase Authentication: Manages user accounts and sign-in
  • Cloud Firestore: Stores conversation history, progress, and user data
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Collects crash reports to improve stability
  • Firebase Performance Monitoring: Monitors app performance metrics

Firebase processes data in accordance with Google's privacy policies. Learn more: Firebase Privacy

3.2 Google Sign-In

When you sign in with Google, we receive basic profile information (email, name, photo) as permitted by Google's OAuth 2.0 protocol. We do not have access to your Google password.

3.3 Apple Sign-In

When you sign in with Apple, we receive basic profile information. Apple may provide you the option to hide your email address using Apple's private relay service.

3.4 Google Gemini AI

We use Google's Gemini API to generate AI tutor responses. Conversation content is sent to Google's servers for processing. Google processes this data according to their Privacy Policy and Generative AI Terms.

3.5 Speech Recognition and Text-to-Speech

We use device-based speech recognition (Android/iOS native) and text-to-speech services to enable voice conversations. Voice data is processed locally or by your device's operating system provider.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Where We Store Your Data

Your data is stored in Firebase Cloud Firestore, which uses Google Cloud Platform data centers. Data may be stored and processed in the United States or other countries where Google operates data centers.

4.2 How We Protect Your Data

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest
  • Authentication: Secure Firebase Authentication with OAuth 2.0
  • Access Control: Strict Firestore security rules limit data access to authenticated users only
  • Firebase App Check: Protects against unauthorized access and abuse
  • Regular Updates: We keep our security measures up to date

4.3 Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained as long as your account is active
  • Conversation History: Stored until you delete conversations or your account
  • Progress Data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Crash Reports: Anonymous crash data retained for 90 days
  • Performance Metrics: Anonymous performance data retained for 90 days

5. Your Privacy Rights

5.1 Access and Portability

You have the right to access your personal data stored in our App. You can view your conversation history, progress, and account information within the App at any time.

5.2 Correction

You can update your profile information (name, photo) through your account settings. Email changes must be done through your sign-in provider (Google/Apple).

5.3 Deletion

You can delete your data in several ways:

  • Individual Conversations: Delete specific conversations from your history
  • All Data: Delete your entire account and all associated data

For complete instructions, see our Account Deletion Guide.

5.4 Withdrawal of Consent

You can stop using the App at any time. If you wish to withdraw consent for data processing, you must delete your account as described above.

5.5 GDPR Rights (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under GDPR:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restriction of processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

6. Children's Privacy

6.1 Age Requirements

Eko is targeted at teens (13+) and adults (parents). While the educational content is appropriate for children ages 8+, accounts must be created by users 13 years or older. Parents are responsible for supervising younger children's use of the App.

6.2 Parental Consent

If you are a parent creating an account to supervise your child's learning, you consent to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy. We recommend that parents:

  • Review all privacy settings
  • Monitor their child's conversations and progress
  • Discuss online safety with their children
  • Use the App together with younger children

6.3 COPPA Compliance (US Users)

We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) by targeting our marketing to teens and adults, not children under 13. Parents create and control accounts used by younger children.

7. Data Sharing and Disclosure

7.1 We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

7.2 Service Providers

We share data only with service providers necessary to operate the App:

  • Google (Firebase): Cloud infrastructure and authentication
  • Google (Gemini AI): AI conversation generation

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for providing services to us.

7.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to:

  • Legal processes (subpoenas, court orders)
  • Government or regulatory requests
  • Protection of our rights or safety of users
  • Investigation of potential violations of our Terms of Service

7.4 Business Transfers

If FlickAI is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred. We will notify you of any such change and provide choices regarding your data.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence, including the United States where Google's servers are located. These countries may have different data protection laws than your country.

For EEA users, we rely on Google's compliance with EU-US and Swiss-US Privacy Shield frameworks and Standard Contractual Clauses for data transfers.

9. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies or web tracking technologies as Eko is a native mobile application. However, Firebase services may use persistent identifiers to provide their services.

We do not use advertising SDKs or tracking for advertising purposes. The App is currently ad-free.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Notify you through the App or via email for material changes
  • Request your consent if required by applicable law

Your continued use of the App after changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.
