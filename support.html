Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Getting Started

Q: How do I create an account?

A: Open the Eko app and tap "Sign In with Google" or "Sign In with Apple" on the welcome screen. Follow the prompts to authenticate with your chosen provider. You must be 13 years or older to create an account.

Q: Is Eko free to use?

A: Yes! Eko is currently completely free with no in-app purchases or subscription fees. All features are available to all users.

Q: What age is Eko suitable for?

A: Eko is designed for teens (13+) and adults. The content is appropriate for children ages 8+ when used with parental supervision. Parents should create accounts and supervise younger children's learning.

Q: What devices are supported?

A: Currently, Eko is available for Android devices (version 5.0 or higher). iOS support may be added in the future.

Using the App

Q: How do I start a conversation with the AI tutor?

A: From the main screen, tap the "Start Conversation" or microphone button. You can choose a conversation topic or let the AI suggest one. Tap the microphone to speak, and the AI will respond with audio and text.

Q: Can I type instead of speaking?

A: The current version of Eko is optimized for voice conversation to help with pronunciation practice. Text input may be added in future updates.

Q: How does the AI understand my pronunciation?

A: Eko uses advanced speech recognition technology (provided by your device's operating system) to convert your speech to text. The AI then provides feedback on grammar, vocabulary, and conversation flow. For best results, speak clearly in a quiet environment.

Q: Can I review my past conversations?

A: Yes! Go to the Profile screen and tap "Conversation History" to view all your past conversations. You can read transcripts, see timestamps, and track your progress over time.

Progress & Achievements

Q: How do I earn XP (Experience Points)?

A: You earn XP by:

  • Completing conversations (10-50 XP based on length)
  • Unlocking achievements (25-100 XP per achievement)
  • Maintaining daily streaks (5-20 XP per day)
  • Learning new vocabulary (5 XP per word)
Q: What are achievements and how do I unlock them?

A: Achievements are milestones you can unlock by reaching specific goals, such as completing your first conversation, maintaining a 7-day streak, or reaching 1000 XP. Check your Profile screen to see all available achievements and your progress toward them.

Q: What happens if I miss a day in my streak?

A: Your streak will reset to zero if you don't use the app for a full day (24 hours). To maintain your streak, complete at least one conversation each day. You can view your current streak in the Profile screen.

Technical Issues

Q: The app says "Microphone permission denied." What should I do?

A: Eko needs microphone access to enable voice conversations. To fix this:

  1. Go to your device Settings
  2. Navigate to Apps ‚Üí Eko
  3. Tap "Permissions"
  4. Enable "Microphone" permission
  5. Restart the Eko app
Q: The AI is not responding or conversations are stuck loading.

A: Try these troubleshooting steps:

  1. Check your internet connection (WiFi or mobile data)
  2. Close and restart the app
  3. Ensure you're signed in to your account
  4. Check if there's an app update available in the Play Store
  5. If the issue persists, contact support with details about the error
Q: The app crashes or freezes frequently.

A: We apologize for the inconvenience. Please try:

  1. Update to the latest version of the app
  2. Clear the app cache: Settings ‚Üí Apps ‚Üí Eko ‚Üí Storage ‚Üí Clear Cache
  3. Restart your device
  4. If crashes continue, email us at myh.public@gmail.com with your device model and Android version
Q: I can't hear the AI tutor's voice responses.

A: Check these settings:

  • Ensure your device volume is turned up
  • Check if the app is muted in your device settings
  • Verify that "Do Not Disturb" mode is not enabled
  • Try using headphones or a Bluetooth speaker
  • Restart the app

Account & Privacy

Q: How do I change my account email or name?

A: Your email is managed by your sign-in provider (Google or Apple). To change your email, you must update it through your Google or Apple account settings. Your display name can be updated in the app's Profile settings.

Q: Is my conversation data private and secure?

A: Yes! Your conversations are stored securely in Firebase Cloud Firestore with encryption. Only you can access your conversation history. We do not share your conversations with third parties except as necessary to provide the AI service (Google Gemini). See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Q: How do I delete my account?

A: See our comprehensive Account Deletion Guide for step-by-step instructions. You can delete your account directly in the app or by emailing us.

Q: Can I export my conversation history?

A: Currently, there is no built-in export feature. If you need a copy of your data, please contact us at myh.public@gmail.com and we can provide your data in a portable format (GDPR right to data portability).

Learning & Education

Q: How does the AI adapt to my skill level?

A: The AI tutor analyzes your conversation patterns, vocabulary usage, and grammar complexity to adjust its responses. As you improve, the AI will gradually introduce more challenging vocabulary and complex sentence structures.

Q: Can Eko help me prepare for English proficiency exams (TOEFL, IELTS)?

A: Eko focuses on conversational English practice, which can help improve your speaking fluency. However, it is not specifically designed for exam preparation. We recommend using Eko as a supplement to dedicated exam preparation courses.

Q: How often should I use Eko for best results?

A: For optimal learning, we recommend daily practice sessions of 10-20 minutes. Consistency is key to language learning! The daily streak feature is designed to encourage regular practice.
